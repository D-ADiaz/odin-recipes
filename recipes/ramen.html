Ingredients
  • Noodles
  • Corn
  • Water
  • Green Onion
  • Pork Broth
  • Some delicious schmeat

Instructions
  1. Put noodles in boiling water
  2. Maybe you heat up the broth first? I don't know
  3. Cook the schmeat
  4. Chop your green onions
  5. Cook your corn and add butter. It is not in the ingredients list
    but you should have it or you are weird.
  6. put your cooked noodles in the broth and throw everything else in too.
  7. Done!
